## FAQ (in case anyone asks)

**Q: Will this change how I sign up for services?**  
A: No, the sign-up process is exactly the same. You just have the option to see future years now.

**Q: What happened to my existing data?**  
A: Nothing! All your sign-ups, songs, and assignments are still there and unchanged.

**Q: Do I have to use the year selector?**  
A: Not at all. If you only work with the current year, you don't need to touch it.

**Q: What if I accidentally click the wrong year?**  
A: Just click the dropdown again and select the correct year. Nothing is changed by viewing different years.
